RFM Analysis

As the new Data Analyst for Meki Group-a global coffee chain headquartered in the USA, your mission is to leverage your analytical skills to address key business questions and provide actionable recommendations for timely and accurate decision-making. To segment customers based on their purchase history, I applied the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis-a behavioral-based method that categorizes customers into segments based on how recently they made a purchase, how often they purchase, and how much they spend.
